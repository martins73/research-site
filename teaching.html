Teaching Philosophy

My goal in the classroom is to bridge the gap between abstract econometric theory and messy, real-world operations. Drawing on my industry experience leading analytics teams at ACHS and Oliver Wyman, I design coursework that emphasizes applicability and intuition over rote memorization.

I believe students learn best when they are asked to solve problems that have no single correct answer—much like the problems they will face in the supply chain of AI.
